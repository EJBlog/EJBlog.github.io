About Our Site

“Sitename” was dreamt up by Evan for the purpose of sharing our stories of life and adventure while advancing knowledge and experiences with webhosting, Jekyll, Liquid, and Github. With the idea fresh on his mind, Evan proposed the idea of creating a website to Jake and here we are.

We always share our crazy ideas with each other in hopes of finding our next project and this site sounded like a winner. We have prior experience with programming from school, work, and other projects, although we wanted to create something, which we genuinely care about and would give us a reason to practice.

We care about learning new things, challenging ourselves in all aspects of life, and having fun while doing it. On this site, we will be sharing stories of our professional and personal experiences with the intention of inspiring or helping other like-minded individuals.

All of the stories seen here are completely our opinion and should taken as so. We are always open to feedback and requests so please feel free to send us a note on our "Contact" page.
